HIRE ME

A graduated cognitive neuroscience Master's student from Radboud University who earned the summa cum laude distinction. Previously worked as a Human Research Technologist for over 3 years studying the mechanisms of visual attention and memory, and 2 years studying the behavioral correlates of Borderline Personality Disorder. Currently working as a Neuroimaging Data Analyst, but seeking a career shift into web development.

Experienced in programming and training others in creating computer experiments for behavioral, eye-tracking, EEG, fMRI, and VR paradigms using various programming languages. This technical foundation has sparked a strong interest in web development, with hands-on experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, React, and Node.js. Proficient in building responsive and dynamic web applications that combine functionality and aesthetics while leveraging modern web technologies to enhance user experience.

Possesses a breadth of experience in all aspects of experimental design, including conceptualizing, implementing, analyzing, and summarizing research studies, and now eager to apply these analytical and problem-solving skills to develop innovative, user-focused web applications.
